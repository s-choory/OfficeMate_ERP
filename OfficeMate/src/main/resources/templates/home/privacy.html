Privacy Policy

At OfficeMate, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your personal information:

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number when you provide it to us.
  • Technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system.
  • Usage data including the pages you visit and the actions you take on our site.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services.
  • To communicate with you regarding your account and our services.
  • To personalize your experience on our site.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Information Sharing

  • We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.
  • We may share your information with service providers who assist us in operating our site and conducting our business.

4. Security Measures

  • We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.
  • We use encryption and other security technologies to safeguard your data.

5. Your Rights

  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • You can object to the processing of your data or request that we restrict its use.
  • You can withdraw your consent to our use of your personal information at any time.

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@officemate.com.
